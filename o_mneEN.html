About me

When I was little, I went to a after school club where we programmed robots from lego. At that time, the film Matrix also came to theaters. I was very fascinated by how you can create a world by writing different characters. But I was led in a different direction. They were martial arts. Eventually, I started doing historical fencing that has held me since 2007. The group I joind in focused on historical theater and mass medieval battles. There I learned to work in a group. Because if you want to win the battle, you have to work together. In 2017, I finally got to do what I really enjoy in fencing. And these are duels with a two-handed sword (HEMA - Historical European Martial Art). I started fencing because of books and games about the Witcher. I consider myself an active player of PC games. In 2019, I became actively interested in working on fireshows, which I have been doing so far. My first real job was paid from sale. Although I still worked part of the working day in simple database programs (eg Siebel 7 adapted for Telekom). I enjoyed working with databases and I enjoyed learning in it until I could do the tasks they knew how to do in the back office. Finally, the wind of fate drove me to the railroad. I became a train driver. But it didn't completely fill me either. But in the end, I got a chance to change my life. I was fired for the corona crisis. It was time to think about what to do next. I only enjoyed two things to do. Either fencing (but it can't be employed) or creating something. Because I enjoyed modifying pc games, I decided to learn some programing language. And that led me to learn the programing of websites.
