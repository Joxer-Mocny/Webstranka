About me

As a little boy, I went to the school club, where we programmed robots from lego. At that time, the film Matrix also came to theaters. I was very fascinated by how you can create a whole world by writing different characters. I started thinking about programming thanks to my hobby - I consider myself an active PC player. I enjoyed modifying my PC games with mods, so programming came naturally. Gradually, I got to creating websites. During life I went through various professions. I worked in sales or as train driver. They all gave me something, but since they weren't fulfilling me, I decided to move on. In addition to programming, I do fencing, specifically dueling with a two-handed sword - HEMA (Historical European Martial Art) and historical theater. Since 2019 I am also actively working with a fire stuff and I also learn how to blow fire as part of a fire show.
